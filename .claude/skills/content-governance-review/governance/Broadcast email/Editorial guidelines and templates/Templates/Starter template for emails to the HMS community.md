# Starter template for emails to the HMS community

*Subject line: [Short subject line summarizing the primary message]*

Dear [recipient] of [recipient affiliation]:

To [realize a benefit to the user], **HMS IT will [user-friendly description of the work] on [date and time].**

[How the work primarily impacts users]. [Why the user should act], **[the primary action the user should take to mitigate the impact]**. [*Optional*: additional actions users must take to mitigate the primary impact.]

[*Optional*: secondary impacts and user actions following the formula above.]

[*Optional*: elaborate on the work and the benefits by describing the problem, solution, and outcome.]

[*Optional*: “For more information” paragraph. Ideally, links will be inserted in the text above where they are most relevant. If this isn’t possible, you can provide additional resources here, like overview pages for affected services or relevant KB articles.]

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT at 617-432-2000 or [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,

HMS Department of Information Technology
