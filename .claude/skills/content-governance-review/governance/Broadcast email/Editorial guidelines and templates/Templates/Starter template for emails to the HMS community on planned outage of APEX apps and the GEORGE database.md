# Starter template for emails to the HMS community on planned outages of APEX apps and the GEORGE database

*Subject line: APEX apps and the GEORGE database unavailable on [dates]*

Dear Users of APEX:

To ensure the security and stability of our applications, Harvard University Information Technology (HUIT) will update the GEORGE database from [dates]. During this time, all [APEX applications](https://it.hms.harvard.edu/apex-applications) will be unavailable.

**Save any work that uses these applications or databases and sign out before the update begins.** Do not access affected services or files during the update.

If you have trouble connecting after the update, restart your computer and try again.

If you have questions or concerns, contact the appropriate support team. If you are affiliated with HMS, contact HMS IT at 617-432-2000 or [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu). If you are affiliated with Wyss, contact [itsupport@wyss.harvard.edu](mailto:itsupport@wyss.harvard.edu). If you are affiliated with HSDM, contact HSDM support at [support@hsdm.harvard.edu](mailto:support@hsdm.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology
