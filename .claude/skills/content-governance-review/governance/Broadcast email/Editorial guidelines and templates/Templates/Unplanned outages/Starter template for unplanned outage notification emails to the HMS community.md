# Starter template for unplanned outage notification emails to the HMS community

*Subject line: [Service] is [unavailable, intermittently unavailable, running slower than usual, and so on]*

Dear Members of the HMS and HSDM Communities:

[State the issue and the scope of the impact on users as precisely as possible.]

[If the issue is internal:] We’re working to resolve this issue as quickly as possible, and we’ll follow up with any critical updates.

[If the issue originates with a third-party vendor:] This issue originates with [vendor name]. HMS IT is monitoring the situation and coordinating with [vendor name] as they work toward resolution. We’ll follow up with any critical updates.

[Provide alternatives to services that are down, if available.]

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT by phone at 617-432-2000 or by email at [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology

## Example

*Subject line: File storage server outage*

Dear Members of the HMS and HSDM Community:

Some file storage servers, including Web and Advanced Research Platforms (WARP) home directories and O2 high-performance computing (HPC) transfer clusters, are inaccessible.

We're working to resolve this issue as quickly as possible, and we'll follow up with any critical updates.

If you need an alternative file storage during the outage, we recommend Dropbox Business.

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT by phone at 617-432-2000 or by email at [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology

## External service example

*Subject line: AWS service disruption affecting research applications*

Dear Members of the HMS and HSDM Communities:

Several HMS research applications hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) are currently unavailable due to a widespread AWS outage affecting multiple U.S. regions.

This issue originates with AWS. HMS IT is monitoring the situation closely and coordinating with AWS as they work toward resolution. We’ll follow up with any critical updates as new information becomes available.

You can check the [AWS service health dashboard](https://health.aws.amazon.com) for the latest updates on the outage.

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT by phone at 617-432-2000 or by email at [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology
