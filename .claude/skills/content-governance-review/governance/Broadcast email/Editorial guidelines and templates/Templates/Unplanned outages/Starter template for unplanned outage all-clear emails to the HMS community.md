# Starter template for unplanned outage all-clear emails to the HMS community

*Subject line: [Service capability restored]*

Dear Members of the HMS and HSDM Communities:

[Notify the user that the issue is resolved. Follow up with specifics about which services are available.]

[Note any caveats or lingering issues that some users may experience.]

[Acknowledge the impact. Don’t use canned language. Be specific about what the impact was to users. For example: “Thank you for your patience as we worked to restore...”]

[If known, explain what caused the issue, with specific times of outages, if that will be useful for the user. Briefly describe steps that will be taken to prevent the issue from recurring.]

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT by phone at 617-432-2000 or by email at [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology

## Example

*Subject line: File server access restored*

Dear Members of the HMS and HSDM Community:

The issue affecting HMS file servers is now resolved. You can now access personal and shared folders and change permissions for files as usual.

We understand how important these servers are for many in the community and that this issue may affected your work. We appreciate your patience and adaptability as we restored access.

We are investigating what caused the issue. We will take what we learn to provide you with more reliable access in the future.

If you have questions or concerns, visit the [HMS IT Service Portal](https://harvardmed.service-now.com/stat). You can also contact HMS IT by phone at 617-432-2000 or by email at [itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu](mailto:itservicedesk@hms.harvard.edu).

Sincerely,
HMS Department of Information Technology
